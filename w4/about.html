Thoughts on Using ChatGPT to Research a Topic

I've never used ChatGPT before this assignment. I was already familiar with what it was, but I had never used it myself. I had a lot of fun playing around with it and trying out different topic ideas. In the video about writing prompts that was linked in the assignment page, they mentioned the ability of chatGPT to pull less-common information or less popular opinions. That was a fun component for me to explore. I have played Valorant for a few years, so I feel like I've heard a lot of tips for improving in the game over time. It was cool to see chatGPT's less common ideas. After playing with it for a while, I can see how intelligent prompt writing can really improve the quality and specificity of the output.
