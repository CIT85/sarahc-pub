My Services

With over a decade of experience in liturgical music and an extensive background as an operatic soprano, I am delighted to offer my services as a singer, pianist, and violinist for your special events, including weddings and funerals. My journey in music has been shaped by a deep passion for both sacred and classical repertoire, allowing me to bring a unique blend of artistry and reverence to every occasion.

As a singer, I specialize in delivering heartfelt and emotive performances that resonate with the solemnity and joy of life's most significant moments. With a versatile vocal range and a repertoire spanning from traditional hymns to contemporary ballads, I work closely with clients to curate a program that reflects their individual preferences and honors the significance of the occasion. Whether it's a stirring rendition of Ave Maria or a soulful interpretation of Amazing Grace, I strive to create a musical atmosphere that uplifts the spirit and provides solace to those in attendance.

My experience in liturgical music has equipped me with a profound understanding of the spiritual significance of ceremonies such as weddings and funerals. I approach each event with sensitivity and reverence, working closely with clients to ensure that the music selected aligns with their cultural and religious traditions. I am committed to providing musical accompaniment that honors the solemnity of the occasion and brings comfort to those in attendance.

While my expertise lies in vocal music, I am proficient in the basics of both violin and piano, capable of adding an elegant and intimate touch to your event. Whether you envision the soft strains of a violin solo during a poignant moment or the gentle accompaniment of a piano, I can provide a an atmosphere that enhaces the experience of your event.

With a deep reverence for the power of music to uplift, inspire, and heal, I am honored to be a part of your special moments. Whether you are celebrating the joy of love and marriage or commemorating the life of a loved one, I look forward to creating a meaningful and memorable musical experience for you and your guests.

Booking

If you'd like to book an event, please reach out to me via email or phone to discuss your event requirements, including the date, location, and musical preferences. During our initial consultation, we'll review your event's timeline, discuss any special requests or accommodations, and determine the appropriate musical repertoire to suit the occasion.

Once we've established the details of your event, I'll provide you with a personalized quote outlining the scope of services and associated costs. This quote will include information on performance fees, travel expenses (if applicable), and any additional services you may require, such as sound equipment rental or rehearsal time.

Upon acceptance of the quote, we'll finalize the booking by signing a contract outlining the terms and conditions of our agreement. This contract will cover important details such as the performance date and time, cancellation policy, and payment schedule.

In the weeks leading up to your event, I'll stay in close communication with you to ensure that all logistical arrangements are in place and that any last-minute adjustments or requests are accommodated. On the day of the event, you can expect me to arrive punctually, fully prepared, and dressed appropriately for the occasion.

Throughout the entire booking process, my goal is to provide you with exceptional service and peace of mind, allowing you to focus on enjoying your special day while I take care of the musical details. Whether you're planning a wedding, funeral, or other significant event, you can trust that I'll go above and beyond to create a memorable and meaningful musical experience for you and your guests.
